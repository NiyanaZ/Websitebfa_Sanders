My Avatar

Interaction Link

Medium: Digital Art Dimensions: 490px by 490px Software: p5js

Although my eyes are usually fixed on the bright side of life, my thoughts are constantly conflicted by my past experiences which hinders me from embracing the optimism that I share with others. I find it easy to share my optimistic perspective on life with others but when it comes to applying that same perspective to my personal life, seeing a bright future becomes a clouded view. The reoccurring clouds infront of me are called angst. Sometimes I can suppress angst and prevent them from dominating my thoughts. Other times, angst wins and that is when their acid rain will fall right where I stand. So as I am in the depths of its leakage my next steps are taken cautiously because of my fear of falling. When my feet become wet I tend to stand still just to make sure that I will not fall. Although I am protecting myself from self inflicted harm I am also doing myself a disservice by not leaping out of faith. Being stagnant gives your life no purpose. This is why I must move even if my feet are wet. It is easy to move when the sun is shining and the ground is dry because you are able to see that the path is safe. However, it is when your path is uncertain and you feel uncomfortable that you should go because that is when you grow and progress. When you fall you get up with more knowledge than before the mistake. So even if the sun has not dried up your path just yet, know that if you keep running toward the light that one day angst will disappear. I wish that Iâ€™d take my own advice.

Character Over Color

Medium: Digital Art Dimensions: 2500px by 1600px Software: Adobe Illustrator x Adobe Photoshop

Martin Luther King Jr is one of my role models. When I think about him the phrase "character over color" comes to mind. Throughout his lifetime he taught everyone who would listen that if they must judge that they should judge people for the content of their character not for the complexion of their skin. This illustration is a tribute to his teachings and something that we should execute today and forever.

I was assigned to take an image of myself that portrays my identity. Unfortunately I lost all ties to my hertiage. All I know is that I am a black woman in America who; loves soul food, family functions and Afrobeats. However, if I could guess where my ancestors came from based on the research I've conducted and the knowledge I've gained during the spand of my life, I'd say that I am a descendant of royalty, warriors and overcomers. This is a depiction of the kind of woman I believe I came from. This the woman I believe I have the potential to be.
